## Concepts

### FHE (Fully Homomorphic Encryption)
- CKKS — 근사 연산
- BGV / BFV — 정수 연산
- TFHE — 비트 단위 연산
- 부트스트래핑 (Bootstrapping)
- 라이브러리: OpenFHE, SEAL, HElib, Lattigo

### ZKP (Zero-Knowledge Proofs)
- Groth16, PLONK, STARK
- Bulletproofs

### MPC (Multi-Party Computation)
- Garbled Circuits
- Shamir Secret Sharing
- Secure Aggregation
